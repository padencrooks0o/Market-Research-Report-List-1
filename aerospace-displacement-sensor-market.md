<p><ul><li>Linear</li><li>Angular</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The aerospace displacement sensor market comprises two primary types: linear and angular sensors. Linear displacement sensors measure motion along a straight path, essential for applications like altitude and distance measurement in various aerospace systems. Angular displacement sensors, on the other hand, assess rotation around a pivot point, critical for monitoring positions in control surfaces and navigation systems. Both types play a vital role in enhancing the precision and safety of aerospace operations, contributing to improved performance in aviation technology.</p></p>
