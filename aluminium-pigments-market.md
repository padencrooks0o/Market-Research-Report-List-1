<p><p>The Aluminium Pigments market comprises two primary types: leafing and non-leafing aluminium pigments. Leafing aluminium pigments are characterized by their ability to float on a surface, creating a metallic sheen, commonly used in coatings, inks, and plastics. In contrast, non-leafing aluminium pigments remain evenly dispersed throughout the medium, providing a uniform metallic appearance without the characteristic gloss, ideal for applications where transparency and even coverage are essential. Both types cater to diverse industrial applications, enhancing aesthetic appeal and functionality.</p></p>
