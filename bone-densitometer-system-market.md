<p><p>The Bone Densitometer System market is categorized into Axial and Peripheral Bone Densitometers. Axial Bone Densitometers, typically used in clinical settings, measure bone density at the spine and hip, providing comprehensive assessments for osteoporosis diagnosis. Peripheral Bone Densitometers, on the other hand, are often portable and assess bone density at the wrist, heel, or finger, making them suitable for screening purposes. Both types serve crucial roles in evaluating bone health and guiding treatment decisions.</p></p>
