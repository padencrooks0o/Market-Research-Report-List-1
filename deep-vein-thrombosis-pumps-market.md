<p><p>Deep Vein Thrombosis (DVT) pumps are specialized devices designed to prevent blood clots in patients, particularly those at risk due to immobility or recent surgery. The market is categorized into lower extremity and upper extremity devices. Lower extremity pumps target the legs, promoting blood circulation to reduce the risk of clot formation in veins of the calves and thighs. Upper extremity pumps are designed for the arms, ensuring circulation in the venous system of the upper body, preventing clots post-surgery or during long periods of inactivity.</p></p>
