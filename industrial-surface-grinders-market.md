<p><p>The industrial surface grinders market includes various types designed for precision grinding applications. Horizontal-spindle grinders use a horizontally mounted spindle for flat surface finishing, ideal for mass production. Vertical-spindle grinders, on the other hand, feature a vertically positioned spindle and are effective for grinding complex shapes. Single disc grinders utilize one rotating disc for efficient surface grinding, while double-disc grinders use two discs to simultaneously grind both sides of a workpiece, enhancing productivity and accuracy in high-volume settings.</p></p>
