<p><ul><li>Strip Cut</li><li>Cross Cut</li><li>Micro Cut</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Paper product shredders are categorized into three main types based on their cutting styles. Strip cut shredders produce long strips of paper, making them suitable for non-sensitive information. Cross cut shredders offer enhanced security by cutting paper into smaller, confetti-like pieces, deterring reconstruction. Micro cut shredders provide the highest level of security, slicing paper into tiny particles, ideal for confidential documents. Each type serves different user needs, balancing security levels and the volume of shredding required in various environments.</p></p>
