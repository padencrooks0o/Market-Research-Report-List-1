<p><p>The Respiratory Infectious Disease Diagnostics Market encompasses several key testing types. Mechanical Tests assess lung function and airflow, often through spirometry. Imaging Tests, such as X-rays and CT scans, visualize respiratory tract abnormalities. In Vitro Diagnostics (IVD) Tests analyze biological samples like blood or sputum to identify pathogens or determine immune responses. Each method plays a crucial role in diagnosing respiratory infections, guiding treatment, and monitoring disease progression, thereby enhancing patient management and outcomes.</p></p>
