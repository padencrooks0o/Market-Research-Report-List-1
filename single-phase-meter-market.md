<p><ul><li>Electromechanical Type Induction Meter</li><li>Electronic Energy Meter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The single-phase meter market comprises two main types: electromechanical induction meters and electronic energy meters. Electromechanical induction meters operate using a rotating disk driven by electromagnetic induction, offering reliability and simplicity but lacking advanced features. In contrast, electronic energy meters utilize digital technology to measure energy consumption accurately, providing enhanced functionalities like remote monitoring and data analytics. The shift toward electronic meters is driven by demand for improved accuracy and energy management capabilities, making them increasingly popular in modern utility applications.</p></p>
